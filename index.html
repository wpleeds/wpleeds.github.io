WordPress Leeds

WordPress Leeds is an informal organisation to help promote WordPress and Blogging events in Leeds and West Yorkshire. Our main event Northern Bloggers, is a monthly meetup for Bloggers, Journalists, Designers, Developers and anyone interested in Blogging and Digital Publishing.

Northern Bloggers is 6:30pm on the 4th Tuesday of the month at Duke Studios. Due to space constraints the event is normally ticketed but is free!

Hosted by the wonderful Duke Studios

Our monthly meetings are hosted by the wonderful folks at Duke Studios!

The address is:

Munro House, Duke St, Leeds, West Yorkshire LS9 8AG

Monro House is a large building, with an Art gallery and coffee shop on the ground floor, Duke Studios is upstairs you will need a code for the lift, which will be displayed and one of the team will be in the lobby until the talks start to assist

How to get there

By Train

The Leeds Central Train Station is a 10 Minute walk from the venue, leave the station via the main concourse doors, turn right and walk down to Boar Lane (Stop in to the wonderful Laynes coffee on the way). Turn right onto Boar Lane and follow till the Corn Exchange, At the corn exchange, take the middle of the three loads at an angle to the exchange. In front of you is the market Turn right and follow the road down to the bottom of the bus station, cross the dual carriage way at the lights and Monro House is on the corner.

By Bus

Leeds main bus station is directly opposite the venue, and has busess coming in from across West Yorkshire as well as the National Express buses from around the rest of the country. From the main bus station, leave the station and turn left, which will take you to the dual carriage way, cross at the crossings Monro house should be just in front of you.

By Car/Bike

Following satnav using the post code LS9 8AG will get you to the back of Munro house, there is pay and display car park behind the venue which is free after 6pm. Very limited on street car parking is available at the side of the venue, the Markets Car Park is less than 5 minutes walk away as well. For cyclists their is a bike rack around the back near the College of Music.

Northern Bloggers Structure & Topics

General Publishing talks

Sessions suitable for all surrounding digital publishing and blogging regardless of platform

WordPress Specific Talks

Sessions specifically talking about feature and concepts related to WordPress

Friendly Chat & Banter

Networking, rants, problem solving and general chatter in between sessions and after.

Normally our monthly Northern Bloggers meetup has two speaker, one General speaker and one WordPress specific. The talks are aimed to be interesting to a broad selection of people, even if you don't use WordPress there is something for you.

We try to publish the speakers and talk topics well in advance, and you can find out more by joining our mailing list or folowing us on twitter.
