WordPress Leeds

WordPress Leeds is an informal organisation to help promote WordPress and Blogging events in Leeds and West Yorkshire. Our main event Northern Bloggers, is a monthly meetup for Bloggers, Journalists, Designers, Developers and anyone interested in Blogging and Digital Publishing.

Northern Bloggers is 6:00pm on the 4th Tuesday of the month at The Test People Offices. Due to space constraints the event is normally ticketed but is free!

What's on this month?

28th April 2015 6pm

The April event is going to be focused on legal issues facing anyone creating things for the web. Whether thats publishing content, or building website. There will be time to mingle and network prior to the talks, and once again we will be going to a nearby Bar afterwards for anyone who wants to join.

Peter Wright - Digital Law for Web Developers and Site Owners

Digital Laws every site owner needs to understand. Peter will be giving a primer on digital laws that effect, bloggers, publishers and site owners the talk will be covering Privacy, controversial cookie law as well as how to collect personal details . What to think about with regards to Web Hosting, sending newsletters and running online stores. It doesn't matter if you are an agency building sites on behalf of clients, or a blogger on a hosted solution Peters talk will guide you through the minefield of potential issues.

DigitalLawUK Ltd is run by Peter Wright, Solicitor and Chair of the Law Society Technology & Law Reference Group at Chancery Lane, London

Digital Law includes the Law relating to websites, copyright, social media networks, intellectual property, data protection, privacy, security and encryption. In the developing online world, Digital Law is the first port of call in governing what can and can’t be done online.

Peter has spoken across the UK on Digital, Data Protection and Social Media Law issues and is working closely with the Law Society on Data Protection Best Practice for Law Firms.

Rob Yorke - Information security a beginners guide

Robs career began back in the early 80s developing computer games during the home computer revolution. He was then involved with the typesetting and reprographic revolution in the mid nineties before moving on-line and began developing websites for some of the first  brands to try this new medium working with clients such as BBC, Disney, Nestle and HSBC.

In recent years he has been involved with a proliferation of new technologies in web marketing some of which have in themselves revolutionised how brands engage with consumers. With his technical background Rob enjoys a hands on approach to projects.

 As development director at Grapple Rob is responsible for ensuring client information and their customers personal data is protected both inside and outside the agency.

Northern Bloggers Structure & Topics

General Publishing talks

Sessions suitable for all surrounding digital publishing and blogging regardless of platform

WordPress Specific Talks

Sessions specifically talking about feature and concepts related to WordPress

Friendly Chat & Banter

Networking, rants, problem solving and general chatter in between sessions and after.

Normally our monthly Northern Bloggers meetup has two speaker, one General speaker and one WordPress specific. The talks are aimed to be interesting to a broad selection of people, even if you don't use WordPress there is something for you.

We try to publish the speakers and talk topics well in advance, and you can find out more by joining our mailing list or folowing us on twitter.

Other Interesting Groups

Local WordPress Groups

WordPress Sheffield

WordPress Leeds little brother, WordPress Sheffield meets on the 2nd Tuesday of every month around Sheffield. They normally run one month social the next talks. Talks tend to be more technical, for more details see WordPress Sheffield website or follow them on Twitter @wpsheffield

WordPress York

Yorkshire newest WordPress group, which started in July 2014, they holding monthly meetings around York. For more details see the WordPress York website or follow them on Twitter @wp_york

Manchester WordPress User Group

Manchester is just an hour from Leeds on the train and home to one of the largest UK user groups run by the co-founder of WordPress Mike Little. Each month MWUG runs both a general and technical session completely devoted to WordPress. For more details see the MWUG website or follow them on Twitter @mwug

Other Leeds & Yorkshire Groups

Leeds PHP

The local PHP user group, who run monthly events around Leeds for more information see Leeds PHP site or follow them on Twitter @leedsphp

Drupal Yorkshire

It's not just WordPress that we are crazy about in Yorkshire we also have an active Drupal community who recently put on their first DrupalCamp (It's like a WordCmp but for Drupal), for more details see Drupal Yorkshire site.

Yorkshire Digital

Want to find a user group in the area, then why not subscribe to Yorkshire Digital email newsletter which each month highlights a few events going around Leeds and the Yorkshire region, to sign up visit Yorkshire Digital you can also follow them on Twitter @yorksdigital
