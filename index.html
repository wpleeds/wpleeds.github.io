WordPress Leeds

WordPress Leeds is an informal organisation to help promote WordPress and Blogging events in Leeds and West Yorkshire. Our main event is a monthly evening meetup for Bloggers, Journalists, Designers, Developers and anyone interested in Blogging and Digital Publishing. We also have a coffee morning for informal chatting.

Our evening event is 6:00pm on the 4th Tuesday of the month at The Digital Garage. Due to space constraints the event is normally ticketed but is free!

What's on this month?

WordPress Leeds - Tuesday 22nd September 2015 6pm

We have two speakers lined up for September's event: Rhys Wynne and Steven Jones.

Rhys Wynne - Telling Better Stories with Aesop

Recently there has been a trend of having pillar, long form content on your site to be individually designed in such a way that stands out and help keep your readers attention. Aesop Story Engine is a plugin that will allow you to mesh rich content such as images, videos and even parallax elements together to create a great user experience. In this talk, Rhys will talk about the features of the plugin, what type of posts Aesop improves, and how to integrate it with your themes.

About Rhys

Rhys Wynne is a WordPress Plugin developer, author and speaker. In 2013 he penned bbPress Complete (ISBN-13: 978-1782167242) and has written plugins that have been downloaded over 100,000 times. Rhys has a keen interest in commercial WordPress development, SEO, security and open source technologies, and enjoys the community that has surrounded open source technologies. He can be found on Twitter at @rhyswynne and plugins, themes and articles on WordPress written by Rhys can be found at Winwar Media.

Steven Jones - Image Optimisation with WordPress

On average images comprise over 2/3rds of the size of a page request. This talk looks at how to optimise images to speed up your website; ranging from image compression to responsive images.

About Steve

Steve is a freelance web developer based in Newcastle upon Tyne with the web presence of Stomp (www.stomptheweb.co.uk). He has been using WordPress for 9 years and hates slow websites.

WordPress Leeds Structure & Topics

General Publishing talks

Sessions suitable for all surrounding digital publishing and blogging regardless of platform

WordPress Specific Talks

Sessions specifically talking about feature and concepts related to WordPress

Friendly Chat & Banter

Networking, rants, problem solving and general chatter in between sessions and after

Normally our monthly meetup has two speakers, one general speaker and one WordPress specific. The talks are aimed to be interesting to a broad selection of people, even if you don't use WordPress there is something for you.

We try to publish the speakers and talk topics well in advance, and you can find out more by joining our mailing list or following us on Twitter.

Other Interesting Groups

Local WordPress Groups

WordPress Sheffield

WordPress Leeds little brother, WordPress Sheffield meets on the 2nd Tuesday of every month around Sheffield. They normally run one month social the next talks. Talks tend to be more technical, for more details see WordPress Sheffield website or follow them on Twitter @wpsheffield

WordPress York

Yorkshire newest WordPress group, which started in July 2014, they holding monthly meetings around York. For more details see the WordPress York website or follow them on Twitter @wp_york

Manchester WordPress User Group

Manchester is just an hour from Leeds on the train and home to one of the largest UK user groups run by the co-founder of WordPress Mike Little. Each month MWUG runs both a general and technical session completely devoted to WordPress. For more details see the MWUG website or follow them on Twitter @mwug

Other Leeds & Yorkshire Groups

Leeds PHP

The local PHP user group, who run monthly events around Leeds for more information see Leeds PHP site or follow them on Twitter @leedsphp

Drupal Yorkshire

It's not just WordPress that we are crazy about in Yorkshire we also have an active Drupal community who recently put on their first DrupalCamp (It's like a WordCmp but for Drupal), for more details see Drupal Yorkshire site.

Yorkshire Digital

Want to find a user group in the area, then why not subscribe to Yorkshire Digital email newsletter which each month highlights a few events going around Leeds and the Yorkshire region, to sign up visit Yorkshire Digital you can also follow them on Twitter @yorksdigital
